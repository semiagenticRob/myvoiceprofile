**Best for:** Someone you've heard on a podcast or seen give a talk — they have a distinct voice and documented opinions.

For podcast guests especially, the use case is obvious: faster first drafts for the writing that comes after the talk — articles, newsletters, LinkedIn posts — in your actual voice.

## Outreach Prioritization

Best segments to hit first:
1. **Newsletter writers** (Template E) — high voice-awareness, tech-forward, most likely to share if they love the product
2. **Blog writers** (Template F) — high volume, volume pressure makes the value prop obvious
3. **Grant writers** (Template D) — highest willingness to pay if positioned correctly, but harder to find via cold DM
4. **Founders / operators** (Template B) — large pool but more skeptical of AI tools
5. **Podcast guests** (Template A) — good for warm outreach when you've heard the episode
6. **Close contacts** (Template C) — best conversion, do these first if you have candidates

Channels by segment:
- Newsletter writers → Twitter/X DM or reply to a recent issue
- SEO/blog writers → LinkedIn DM or Indie Hackers
- Grant writers → LinkedIn DM (search "grant writer" + nonprofit sector)
- Founders → LinkedIn or email
