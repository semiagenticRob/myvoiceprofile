Common questions

What kind of links can I send?

YouTube videos (interviews, talks, podcasts you've appeared on), podcast episodes, blog posts, newsletter issues, published articles — anything where you're speaking or writing in your authentic voice. Auto-captioned YouTube videos work great.

Which AI tools does the prompt work with?

Any of them — Claude, ChatGPT, Gemini, Perplexity, whatever you already use. The Voice Starter Prompt is plain text. Paste it at the top of any conversation and the AI inherits your voice for that session.

What if I don't have many interviews or articles yet?

Five solid pieces is enough to produce a useful profile. Three long-form interviews or podcast appearances will get you further than ten short social posts. Depth matters more than volume.
